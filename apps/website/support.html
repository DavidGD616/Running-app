Account & Authentication

How do I sign up or log in?

You can create an account using your email address or sign in with Google. Open the StrivIQ app, tap "Get Started," and follow the prompts to set up your account.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. We'll send you a reset link. If you signed in with Google, manage your password through your Google account settings.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data, including training plans and run history. This action cannot be undone.

Training Plans

How are my training plans generated?

StrivIQ creates personalized training plans based on your goal distance, target date, current fitness level, and weekly availability. Plans adapt as you complete sessions and log runs.

Can I skip a session?

Yes. In your plan view, tap the session you want to skip and select "Skip." The plan will automatically adjust future sessions to keep you on track toward your goal.

GPS Runs

What location permissions does StrivIQ need?

StrivIQ needs "While Using the App" location access to track your runs. On iOS, you can also grant "Always" permission if you want background tracking for longer runs, but this is optional.

Does StrivIQ track runs in the background?

Yes, StrivIQ can continue tracking your run even when the app is in the background or your phone is locked. Make sure location permissions are set to "Always" if you want this feature.

What happens if the app crashes mid-run?

If the app crashes during a run, your progress up to that point is usually saved locally. When you reopen the app, you may be prompted to resume or save the partial run. If data appears missing, contact support.

Devices & Sync

Does StrivIQ support Apple Watch?

Apple Watch support is on our roadmap. For now, you can track runs using your iPhone's GPS and health sensors. Stay tuned for updates.

Does StrivIQ use Live Activities?

Live Activities support is planned for a future update. You'll be able to see your current run stats directly on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island.
